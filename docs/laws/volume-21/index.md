**Period Covered:** 1978-1980  
**Historical Context:** Late military administration and transition to civilian rule

## Overview

This volume covers the transition period from military administration under General Ziaur Rahman to the restoration of civilian government. It includes significant political reforms, institutional developments, and the beginning of democratic restoration processes.

## Historical Significance

- **Political Transition:** Formation of political parties and democratic institutions
- **Institutional Development:** Establishment of key regulatory and oversight bodies
- **Economic Reforms:** Foreign investment promotion and financial sector development
- **Administrative Modernization:** Government service reforms and administrative oversight
- **Parliamentary Return:** Gradual restoration of parliamentary democracy
